Pricing Details

Free Plan

Trello’s Free plan is perfect for individuals and small teams who are just getting started with project management. It includes unlimited cards, up to ten boards per workspace, and essential features like Power-Ups, basic automation, and file attachments up to 10MB. It’s simple, flexible, and free forever — ideal for organizing personal projects or small collaborations.

Standard Plan

The Standard plan helps growing teams collaborate more effectively. It offers unlimited boards, advanced checklists, custom fields, and more automation options. With additional views like calendar and dashboard, teams can plan and track work more clearly. It’s designed for those who want to stay organized and productive without complexity.

Premium Plan

The Premium plan is built for teams that need to manage multiple projects and visualize work in different ways. It includes all Standard features plus timeline, table, and map views, unlimited automation, and advanced admin controls. This plan is best for teams looking to scale their workflow and gain more control over project tracking.

Enterprise Plan

The Enterprise plan is designed for large organizations that need powerful security, control, and flexibility. It includes all Premium features plus organization-wide permissions, advanced admin tools, and enhanced data protection. With centralized management and unlimited workspaces, Enterprise ensures smooth collaboration across departments.
